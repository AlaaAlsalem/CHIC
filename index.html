Investors / entrepreneur Immigration

Canada is one of the most desired destination for starting businesses and investment in the world, the political and economic stable system put Canada in a well reputation country, in addition to the magnificent social fabric where the multiculturalism is an official policy of the state. The laws and rules in Canada create a secure environment for investing and a quality life level. CHIC has a medley of the professionalism in immigration law and business, that gives CHIC a gigantic ability to advise, represent, and achieves the goals for its clients in their economic immigration applications and their businesses in Canada. For more information, please contact CHIC

Family Class Immigration

CHIC has the Knowledge and the experience with the IRCC Spousal Sponsorship, and it is the key to success, we can proudly say that we have both. Representing the refugee claimants who apply for refugee from within Canada is one of the most focused fields that we work on. The experience in refugee issues, claims, and cases empowers CHIC the ability and the high reputation in representing refugees to IRB. We are proud that we achieved high numbers of approved refugee claims, and all were done in a professional and ethical way






Study in Canada

Canada is one of the best destinations for study because of many reasons; the high-quality education at an affordable cost, a safe community, high- tech campus and high-tech country, its rich multiculturalism, the high possibility of immigrating. Study in Canada is one of the most effective ways to immigrate and one of the strategic pathways to establish yourself in Canada and make it your permanent home as Canadian. CHIC has its own specialized team who works hard to meet your expectations and beyond. Our team will guide you through the all stages, from choosing the program, the city, and securing the acceptance letter, to the stage of applying for study permit and visa until arriving and we gladly offer after arriving services.

Visitor and tourism Visa

CHIC has a wide experience in applying for temporary resident visa (TRV), for visit, tourism, or explore the country for business purposes.
We deal with refused application and identify the reasons behind rejected applications. CHIC advises you about the right way to apply for your visa, and we work together to make your visa application is a successful one.







Provincial Nominee Programs

Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) streams are an essential anchor of Canada’s immigration policy and trends, it is highly expected to increase the numbers of Canadian permanent residence through a Provincial Nominee Program by 2023. Provincial Nominee Programs are growing significantly, it includes different sub categories under its umbrella, for who have Canandian experience, study in Canada, investors, and even from outside Canada.
