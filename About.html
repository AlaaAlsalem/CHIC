About Us

Canadian Hub Immigration Consultation

Welcome to Canadian Hub Immigration Consultation website! We are an immigration consulting firm located in GTA, Ontario, Canada. We provide a professional service in various aspects of Canadian immigration programs and pathways to individual and corporate clients worldwide.

Our firm handles all wide range of immigration applications and legally represent our clients’ interests before Immigration, Refugee and Citizenship Canada – IRCC, Immigration and Refugee Board of Canada – IRB, and Canada Border Services Agency – CBSA, working with inland as well as oversees processing, including but not limited to such immigration categories and programs as:

  • Business Immigration: Federal and Provincial
  • Business Immigration: Federal and Provincial
  • Family Class Sponsorship
  • Provincial Nominees: skilled workers, entry level, and business immigrants
  • Temporary status: temporary resident visas, Super Visa, and Study Permits
  • Temporary Resident Status Extensions
  • Temporary Resident Status Restoration
  • Temporary Resident Permits
  • Express Entry immigration streams: Federal Skilled Worker, Canadian Experience, Federal Skilled Trades
  • Applications on Humanitarian and Compassionate Grounds
  • Permanent Resident Card – renewal
  • Applications for Canadian Citizenship

Our everyday goal is to achieve successful outcomes to our clients, by advising the most appropriate solution. If you would like to know whether you could be eligible to immigrate to Canada, we invite you to complete our online assessment questionnaire. The information you provide will enable us to assess your qualifications in accordance with the Citizenship and Immigration Canada’s current selection criteria. All information provided to us will be held in strict confidence in compliance with the Rules of Professional Conduct of CICC. If you are interested in seeking professional assistance to guide you through the immigration process or if you have any questions, please contact us. Each case and its circumstances are unique, and each file will be processed by our firm is treated as such. Please note that the information provided on this website is meant for general information purposes only and not as legal advice and you agree that it will not be relied upon as such.
